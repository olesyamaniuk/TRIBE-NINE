GAME DESCRIPTION

TRIBE NINE takes you into a dark and chaotic future where Neo Tokyo is ruled by absolute madness.
A ruthless dictator, the masked man Zero, seeks to reshape the world into his own vision of power and control.
You are one of the last rebels, a fighter who refuses to submit.
Form your tribe, master the art of futuristic combat, and challenge the corrupt forces that govern this dystopian world.
Will you rise as the ultimate warrior and reclaim Neo Tokyo, or will you fall to the chaos?
